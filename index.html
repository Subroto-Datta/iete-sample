Renaissance 2025

IETE KJSIT's Arena of Innovation

Get ready to plug in! For this year's annual tech festival, IETE KJSIT is unleashing a supercharged lineup of events. We're transforming Renaissance into a battleground of brilliance where code meets creativity. Whether you're here to conquer complex circuits or outsmart the competition in challenges of pure wit, we've built an arena for you. The future is calling—are you ready to answer?

Technical Events

Code Reverse

Participants will be given the output and must write the code to produce it within a strict time limit. A true test of logic and efficiency.

Prompt Showdown

An AI vs. AI challenge! Given a problem, participants compete using AI tools like ChatGPT to generate the best story, design, or solution. The most creative and effective output wins.

Digital Sherlock

Solve a series of "digital crime" scenarios by finding clues hidden in fake emails, image metadata, websites, or QR codes. A cyber-forensics challenge for teams.

Code Golf

The ultimate test of brevity. Solve a problem using the shortest possible code. Like golf, the lowest score (fewest characters) wins. Play solo or in teams of up to 4.

Non-Technical Events

IPL Auction (Simulator)

Step into the shoes of a franchise owner. Strategize, bid, and build your dream team in this thrilling simulation of the iconic cricket auction.

Gaming Zone

Unleash your competitive spirit on the PS5 with high-octane FIFA matches and blistering F1 races. Settle scores and claim bragging rights on the virtual field and track.

Alice in Borderland

Enter a world of high-stakes games across four unique rooms:
Blindfolded Maze: Navigate a maze guided only by your teammate's voice.
Mafia: Uncover the hidden mafia before they take over.
Housie: A classic game of luck and patterns.
Geometric Dumb Charades: Act out geometric terms without a sound.

SOCIAL BUGS

A trio of challenges designed for social media masters:
Reel o Mania: Create a viral 15-30 second reel using mystery objects.
Aamchi Mumbai: A rapid-fire quiz about the city of dreams.
MemoLogic: A fun challenge to test your memory and logic skills.

Our Story

The Genesis of Innovation at KJSIT

Born from a vision to create a hub for technical excellence, the IETE KJSIT chapter was founded by a small group of passionate students and faculty. Our initial goal was simple: to bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world application, creating a space where curiosity could thrive and innovation had no limits.
